About

Whip’s founders have ridden thousands of miles on electric scooters. They had a glimpse of the future, but they were disappointed by the absence of build quality, performance, safety and desirable, thoughtful design. For people to start using light electric vehicles, they need to be all of these things.

Whip Transportation is a micromobility company building a replacement for the car in the city. Car ownership has benefitted from high touch servicing, risk mitigation and thoughtful design for over a century. It’s only right that the same quality and service are offered to light electric vehicle users.

A Whip gives you the confidence to ride on the road and park on the street. Whip services mitigate the risk of owning a premium light electric vehicle and guarantee its uptime.

The result is a fast, dependable and effortless way to get around the city. A Whip is like a car, minus the traffic.

Why Whip?

‘Whip’ is another word for car.

Our mission is to provide the freedom, dependability and convenience you’d expect from a car in the city, but without the dead weight that leads to congestion.

Can I buy a Whip?

When you choose Whip, you’re choosing the right side of history. Less congestion, shorter journey times, less pollution and more efficient use of fuel.

Micromobility users deserve an exceptional experience. We believe a subscription service is the best way to deliver this experience to as many people as possible.

By taking responsibility for the vehicle, we are incentivised to make them last longer whilst
mitigating the risks associated with using a light electric vehicle for our customers.

Unlimited servicing, an uptime guarantee and continuous product development are just some of the benefits Whip customers receive as part of their subscription.
